LET THE DICE DECIDE

Have you ever spent 10 minutes deciding what to eat?

Maybe you're reading too many books, and don't know which one to continue with?

Or maybe you can't decide if you should go that Chinese place or the Italian one?

Life is full of these trivial decisions. And we're here to help!

IT TAKES 3 STEPS:

1. Choose a decision. Ideally, this is a daily decision you will have to make often

2. Create a new dice with a title and description of what the dice is for.

3. Add the dice faces (options). You can have as many as you like!

Now you are ready to roll!

ps. to edit the dice or save for easy access, please sign up for an account.
